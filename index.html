Overview

Newrains International Journal of Applied and Pure Mathematics is a platform for original research in all areas of pure and applied mathematics, statistics, and related areas.

  • Offers selected research in Pure and Applied Mathematics and Statistics.
  • Extends coverage to Operations Research, Mathematical Economics, Mathematics Biology, and Computer Science.
  • Official publication of the Newrains.
  • Enjoys international reputation and wide distribution.
  • Committed to publishing research of interest to broad sections of the mathematical community."
